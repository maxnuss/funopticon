Funopticon is a weekend of fun & games, bonding, debauchery, grammar lessons, highbrow philosophy, antitrust debates, and lawnmowing*. Think of it as being somewhere in between summer camp for adults and a music festival minus the music.

* Must provide your own lawnmower.

Funopticon will be held from August 13–16, 2020, at Camp Berkshire Hills Eisenberg in Copake, NY.
