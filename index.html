About

Funopticon is a weekend of fun & games, bonding, debauchery, and fast cars. Think of it as being somewhere in between summer camp for adults and a music festival minus the music.

*No fast cars.

Location & Date

Funopticon will be held from September 6–8, 2019, at Camp Echo Lake in Warrensburg, NY.

It’s about a four-hour drive from NYC and a one-hour drive from Albany. We will be coordinating optional transportation to and from both cities.

Tickets

To make Funopticon as accessible as possible, we are pricing tickets on a sliding scale:

  • Job Creators ($250): Cover your costs + a $60 contribution to help others attend Funopticon.
  • Patriots ($190): Cover your costs.
  • Peaceniks ($100): Discount ticket if $190 is too much for you.

There are a limited number of $100 tickets available, so please only take one if you really need it (honor system here).

Also, these prices include the deposit we’re required to hold with the camp. If we don’t break anything, all Job Creators and Patriots will receive around $20 back after the event.

FAQ

Can I bring a friend/my significant other/my parents?

We expect that most attendees will be able to bring at least one guest, but we won’t know for sure until first wave ticket sales close at the end of February.

Are you guys making money on this?

No, we’re running Funopticon at cost. We might even lose money!

Why is it called Funopticon?

The Panopticon is a type of institutional building and a system of control designed by the English philosopher and social theorist Jeremy Bentham in the late 18th century. This is like that, but fun.
